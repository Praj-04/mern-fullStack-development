Bonpastry is owned & operated as a family business by Sergio Stephano. Our pastry chef began baking at a young age in Paris, France and learned the art of baking and preparing tantalizing French pastries. Bonpastry was inspired by experiences exploring the dessert world of Cannes in the French Riviera. Our family's love for pastries motivated her to share her baking expertise with friends and family.We only use seasonal ingredients
